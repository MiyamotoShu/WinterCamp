Cryperpunk is a 2.5D RPG game set in a cyberpunk world that combines Web3 blockchain technology, allowing players to truly own their in-game assets, breaking away from traditional Gamefi models and creating a new gaming community.


The game will be presented in a free and rich manner, allowing players to mix and match skills and attack weapons to create their own unique playstyle. Different quest givers will offer diverse missions in the game, and players can obtain equipment materials and NFT skins while completing missions, earning relative rewards in return. Character appearance can be freely matched with NFT skins, making players stand out and look cool. We will also provide a personal web page to showcase your character and any unique NFTs you own.

Whitepaper

Cryperpunk introduces a global sharing economy model to the gaming industry. Players can contribute their ideas and skills to the game design and story, and share in the profits, creating a more diverse and empowered gaming community. This decentralized approach to gaming offers a unique and innovative gamefi experience. Join us in shaping the future of gaming.


In the future, we will create more games and make the NFTs obtained in the game usable in these games, building a new world belonging to everyone.
